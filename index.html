The Marburg Brainhack 2019 is part of the Brainhack Global 2019 . The goal of the hackathon is to bring together researchers with disparate backgrounds to collaborate on open science projects in neuroimaging.

Dates: November 13-15, 2019

Costs: completely free (through the support of our awesome sponsors)! You'll get onsite breakfast, lunch and refreshments

Location: Marburg, Germany.

Participants are encouraged to post project ideas to www.brainhack.org and to submit a report describing their project to the 2018 Brainhack Proceedings that will be published by Gigascience after the event.

Please join the Brainhack Slack team for updated information on the developing hackathon content and to contribute your own ideas.
